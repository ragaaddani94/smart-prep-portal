Interview Tips

The STAR Method

Structure your behavioral answers using Situation, Task, Action, and Result for maximum impact.

Research the Company

Go beyond the home page. Understand their product, culture, and recent challenges.

Active Listening

Focus on understanding the interviewer's intent before jumping into an answer.

Reverse Interviewing

Have 3 thoughtful questions ready for the interviewer to show your deep interest.

Resume Strategies

Quantify Results

Use numbers like "Increased revenue by 20%" instead of "Worked on sales".

Keyword Optimization

Align your skills with the job description to pass Applicant Tracking Systems.

Whitespace Matters

Keep your layout clean and readable. Less is often more when it comes to design.

One-Page Rule

Unless you have 10+ years of experience, stick to a concise single-page format.

Bug Fixing Workflows

Binary Search Debugging

Isolate issues by commenting out halves of your code to find the failing section.

Console & Logs

Master your browser's dev tools and server-side logging for real-time insights.

Rubber Ducking

Explain your code line-by-line to someone (or something) to find logical flaws.

Version Control

Use git bisect to identify exactly which commit introduced the regression.
